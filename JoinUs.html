‚ôü Activities

---There are many sorts of activities in this club. In general meetings, Students get to challenge an opponent on an over-the-board game or online game. However some days there are different game plans. On lesson days, a specific tactic or opening is used and thoroughly studied by the students. Duuring club tournaments, students participate in a knockout bracket chess tournament with prizes for the winners. During challenges, a powerful player is picked and must play against all participating students to see how many games they can win simultaneously.

---In "Varients" Chess, students either do a small tournament or a match but instead of normal Chess, a varient is played (eg. 4 Player, Atomic, Duck, 960, Crazyhouse, etc). There are some days where students don't play. In cases where a student is going to a national or an international tournament, there would be a field trip hand picked to allow students to go to support the accepted student. There are also free yearly field trips in which students go and meet famous grandmasters to get advice.

---Finally, there are guest visit days when titled masters communicate or teach students about chess either in-person or through Zoom. These are the hardest ones to coordinate.
Our leaders have worked hard to all of make this possible. So take part in all of it and have fun.
