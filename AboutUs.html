Brief History About Chess Club

Chess club here at Monterey Trial has been established since the 1850s and we are dedicated to continuing on the legacy. Our original president, Paul Morphy, was filled with a wonderful cabinet that worked alongside himself which includes...

  1. Magnus Carlsen (Secretary)
  2. Bobby Fischer (Treasurer )
  3. Garry kasparov (Historian)
  4. Arjun Erigaisi (Publicity)

They have built the club from the zero to hero to what we have today. Our original president and the cabinet team were passionate students who believed chess was more than a game and went on to win many local chess competitions. It is their amazing coordination that Chess Club has managed to have international fame, and with that, WE are the Chess Club at Monterey Trial High School
