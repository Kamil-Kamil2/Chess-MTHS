Chess is the game we all know and love. It is important we understand the fundementals of the game if you plan on playing it.
Here are a couple sources that make playing or learning Chess easy.

  • Chess.com

    Chess.com is the Worlds #1 Chess website in the World. It is known for its massive Community and its Unique tools.
    It is in our opinion the Best website to start to learn Chess or play games with because of its simplicity.

  • LiChess.org

    While not as widely used as Chess.com, LiChess is another great online chess website.
    While it may lack in the diversity and simplicity Chess.com has,
    it is better for its AD free interface. Lichess also allows you to play Many Chess variants, unique variations of chess, for free.
